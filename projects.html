Projects || Album Showcase

Wishful Thinking By Benches

This album is fairly new to me and really so is this band. I only started listening about a month ago, but it just captivated me. I really enjoyed that sound of the lead singer and way that they played. It has a very similar feel to The Strokes, which is another of my favorite artists to listen too. At first, my favorite track from this was the first one, "Angry Lizard Noise", but the more that I listened, one of the later tracks called, "Yes, Please, Don't", took that spot as my favorite.

Am I sinking or Am I swimming? - late night drive home

This EP has been one of my most played over the past 2 weeks for a few reasons. This EP samples one of my favorite artists, "Her's" and their song "Harvey". Hearing that be used on the first track of this EP was something that instantly hooked me into the rest. While this isn't technically a album, it still felt like it deserved a spot on this project. My favorite on this EP is the fourth track, "Nostalgic Love Rock" The lyrics just feel nice to listen to along with the switching beat over the chorus.

In Rainbows - Radiohead

While I think that another album from Radiohead has more consitency within its tracks I can't help, but love "In Rainbows" for the few on here that stand out from the rest of Radiohead's Discography. The first song that immediately grabbed my attention and had addicted was "Jigsaw Falling into Place". I found this song at a perfect time in my life. It talks about the rush of emotions when meeting a new person. How social interactions can be used to take out the stress of our daily lives. How sometimes when you just don't understand where your life is going, that it all is apart of a bigger picture and it all falls into place. Other notable tracks from this album are: Weird Fishes, 15 Steps, All I Need, and bodysnatchers.

Twin Fantasy - Car Seat Headrest

I believe that this album in particular is something that must be listened to multiple times before you can understand it. I have personal experience from this as on my first listen I completely misunderstood the point of it. I really was listening only for the interesting instrumentals I found on the tracks: Bodys and Beach Life-in-Death. After going through it again and really listening to the lyrics, its not as upbeat as the instrumentals would have you feel. My favorite track still stayed, "Beach Life-In-Death" on this album. It is not a easy listen sitting at over 14 minutes long, it talks about the struggles of not fitting in, the idea that you aren't needed, and the lack of help people who are truly suffering don't recieve.
